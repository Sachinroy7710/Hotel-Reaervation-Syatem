Overview

Feel like you're a part of something special when you're in town when you stay at Bluebird Lake Placid. Let this hotel be your home base, jumping off point, and relaxing space to enjoy before all the action starts right outside your door.

Discover a New Boutique Hotel in the Heart of Lake Placid

Bluebird Lake Placid is a brand new hotel on Saranac Avenue in Lake Placid, within walking distance of Mirror Lake. The 90 rooms and suites are designed with Scandinavian and Japanese influences for an ultra modern, chic feel. Whether you're traveling just the two of you or bringing the whole family, you'll find accommodations to suit everyone's style.

Uncover a Four-Season Destination of Adventures

Being in the middle of Lake Placid means being where all the action is! From dog sled rides over a frozen Mirror Lake to hiking in the Adirondack mountains, there is an activity for everyone. Slide down the 30-foot long Lake Placid Toboggan Chute that's only open in the winter, embark on a hike on the Northville-Lake Placid Trail, hit a few rounds of golf at the Lake Placid Club, or visit the Olympic Museum, a mere 3-minute drive or 12-minute walk from the hotel. These are just a few suggestions for nearby activities!
